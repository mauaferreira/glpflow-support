Privacy Policy — GlpFlow (iOS)

Last updated: 17/12/2025

GlpFlow respects your privacy and was built with transparency and full user control over personal data. This Policy explains how information is handled when using the app.

1. General Information

GlpFlow is a habits and wellness tracking app that operates offline-first, without account creation, without login, and without external servers for storing personal data.

2. Data Collection and Storage

GlpFlow does not collect personal data on servers, does not use cloud storage, and does not transmit information to third parties.

All data entered by the user — such as habits, daily logs, wellness-related information, and preferences — is stored exclusively on the user’s device using local storage.

2.1 Local Storage Limitations

By using local storage only, the user acknowledges that:

  • No data is sent to external servers or cloud services;
  • Data is not synced across devices;
  • Data cannot be transferred when switching devices;
  • There is no automatic backup;
  • In case of loss, theft, formatting, or device replacement, data will be permanently lost;
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data;
  • Each device works in isolation.

By using GlpFlow, the user agrees to these technical limitations.

3. Apple Health Integrations

GlpFlow may integrate with Apple Health only with the user’s explicit authorization. Information accessed or recorded via this integration remains stored locally on the device and is not shared with third parties.

4. Information Sharing

GlpFlow does not share personal data with third parties, advertisers, commercial partners, or external services.

4.1 No Data Transmission

The app does not transmit, send, or store information in APIs, servers, external services, or cloud infrastructure. All content remains exclusively under the user’s control on the device.

5. Subscriptions and Payment Information

GlpFlow may offer Premium features via paid subscriptions. Payments are processed exclusively by the device’s app store, and GlpFlow does not collect or store financial data such as credit card or bank information.

We receive only limited subscription status information (active, expired, or canceled), used solely to enable or restrict Premium features.

6. Information Security

Because data is stored locally, security also depends on the user’s own measures, such as device passcode, biometrics, and keeping the operating system up to date.

The developer does not have access to stored data and cannot recover it in case of loss.

7. Data Deletion

The user can delete all locally stored data at any time using the “Delete all data” option available in the app. This action is irreversible and permanently removes all stored information.

8. Changes to this Policy

This Policy may be updated periodically. Any relevant change will be informed within the app. Since no data is collected on servers, no external notifications are sent.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us using the channel listed on the App Store page or email: contato@i9techia.com.br.
